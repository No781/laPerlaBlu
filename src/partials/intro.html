WELCOME

The Bistro La Perla Blu in Basel offers something to satisfy every hunger. We treat you in our rustic locale with Pizze, Pasti and a sortiment of specialities from Sicily. Wochentags bietet das La Perla Blu eine "gutbürgerliche Gourmetküche" in ruhiger Atmosphäre.
